§ 02 · The Problem

Rotate.
Don't slide.

A good golf swing rotates around a stable axis. A bad one slides. The hips drift toward the target, the spine tilts late, the head chases the body — and the ball goes wherever it happens to be at impact. Every golfer has been told to "keep your head still," and every golfer has found out how useless that advice is without a reference.

SteadyEyeScope gives you the reference. Fix the ball inside the sight ring at address, and any head movement during the swing is visible instantly: the ball leaves the frame. If the ball stays in the ring, you rotated. If it slides out the back or the front, so did you. A physical answer to a physical question, delivered the moment you finish your swing.

§ 03 · The Method

Three movements.

  1. Mvt. 01

    Mount

    Lay the instrument flat on the mat or turf. Extend the shaft until the sight ring floats above your ball, then lock the tightener. The scope is calibrated to your stance in under a minute.

  2. Mvt. 02

    Address

    Frame the ball dead-center of the sight ring. The ring now marks your stable axis. Your job for the next two seconds is to rotate around it without letting the ball leave the frame.

  3. Mvt. 03

    Rotate

    Take it back, turn through, finish tall. If the ball stays in the ring, you rotated around a steady head. If it drifts forward you slid your hips; if it drifts back you swayed off it. The scope tells you which, immediately.

§ 05 · The Questions

Answered.

Is this legal in tournament play?
No. SteadyEyeScope is a training instrument, intended for the range, the practice green, and the mat in your garage. Governing bodies prohibit training aids during stipulated rounds, as they should.
What is in the box?
The scope assembly, swivel socket, adjustable reference shaft, tightening hardware, and a cotton dust pouch. No tools are required for setup.
Left-handed?
Yes. The swivel base rotates a full 360°. The sight line adjusts to either side without hardware changes.
Where does it ship from?
The United States. Domestic orders ship within three business days. International shipping is available on request — write us.
